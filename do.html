Museums and Historical attractions

Irviton House Park and Museum

Locaated just outside the heart of dowtown Richmond is the Irvinton House and Park. Left to the City after the death of Elizabeth Irvine she stipulated that all furniture and belongings be left and the home to be left to the medical society and was used as a hostpital for many years.
Now functioning as a city park and the home is about for tours.

White Hall State Historic Site

The White Hall State Historic site is nestled on rolling acres of farm land just on the edge of madison county. White Hall is the home that was in the Clay politcal family for many generations. The home was the first of many to have modern ammnenties for the time such as an indoor pluming system and indoor heating.
Now under the managment of Eastern Kentucky University, tours are avalible of the home and the property offers many walking trails as well as playground and pond.
