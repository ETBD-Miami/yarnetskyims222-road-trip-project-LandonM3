Great Local Places to Eat in Richmond

Richmond has many great local places that should be checked out on your visit!

Newby Country Store

Established in 1891, The Newby Country Store is one of the last true country stores left in the state. Serving handmade before you sandwiches, snack and a glass beverage to wash it all down with. You can also fill up your tank with the one gas pump out front. Newby Country Store is a must see.

    • Winks

    Located on Water street of downtown Richmond is Winks Bar is a local resturant that is locally owned and operated and is known to serve "cheap booze and great food till 1:00 am". A hole in the wall and is a must see.

    Apollo Pizza

    Apollo Pizza

    Locally owned and operated since the 1980's, Apollo pizza has been voted best pizza in Madison County five years in row and featured on food critic Alton Browns Road Eats. Apollo is a Downtown Richmond Staple and host's the annual Hops and Vine Festival featuring live music and local vendors. Apollo is a must stop and is known for their pizza and garlic breadsticks.

    El Mejor

    Locally owned and operated is EL Mejor Mexican Resturant. Translated to english, "The Best", it quiet literally is. Enjoy an array of Mexican style dishes and wash it down with a margaitta from the bar. This resturant truly stands out from the vast majority of growing mexican restruants in town with it's great food and wonderful hospitallity that makes you feel at home.

    Purdy's Coffee Shop

    Richmond's very own lcoally owned coffee shop is Purdy's. Now operating in two locations, one in the heart of downtown and another on big hill avenue. Purdy's provides you with the perfect beverage and snack selection and a great venue for studying or catching up with friends.
