Great Local Places to Eat in Richmond

Richmond has many great local places that should be checked out on your visit!

Newby Country Store

Established in 1891, The Newby Country Store is one of the last true country stores left in the state. Serving handmade before you sandwiches, snack and a glass beverage to wash it all down with. You can also fill up your tank with the one gas pump out front. Newby Country Store is a must see.
