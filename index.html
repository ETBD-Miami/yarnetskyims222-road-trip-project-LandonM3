Why Richmond?

Richmond Kentucky was founded in 1798 and shows through it's many historical sights and attractions. Richmond is home to Eastern Kentucky University and is considered home to nearly 37,000 residents.
Richmond is the thrid largest city in the Bluegrass region and the state of Kentucky's sixth largest city. Conventiently located thirty mintues south of Lexington, Richmond provides you with many wonderful sights and attractions with the perfect balance of big city attractions with small town comfort.
